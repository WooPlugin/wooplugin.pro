## Overview

PDF reports provide professional, shareable accessibility compliance documentation. They're designed for agencies delivering to clients, organizations building legal compliance records, and anyone needing a formal accessibility audit document.

This is a Pro feature. [Upgrade to Pro](https://wooplugin.pro/accessibility-scanner) to generate PDF reports.

## Generating a Report

1. Run a scan (single page or full site crawl)
2. Go to **Accessibility → Dashboard**
3. Click **Generate PDF Report**
4. Configure report options
5. Click **Download**

## Report Contents

Each report includes:

### Executive Summary

- Overall accessibility score
- Total pages scanned
- Issue breakdown by severity (errors, warnings, notices)
- Compliance status summary

### Detailed Findings

- Issues organized by WCAG category
- Each issue with severity, affected element, and remediation guidance
- WCAG success criterion reference for each finding
- Page-by-page breakdown (for full site reports)

### Recommendations

- Prioritized list of fixes ranked by impact
- Estimated effort level for each fix
- Quick wins highlighted

### Compliance Statement

- Current WCAG compliance level
- Target compliance level
- Date of assessment
- Assessor information

## Customization

### Branding

Add your organization's branding to reports:

- **Logo** — Upload your company logo
- **Company name** — Displayed on the cover page
- **Colors** — Customize the accent color
- **Contact info** — Your company's contact details

### White-Label (Agency)

Agency license holders can fully white-label reports:

- Remove all WooPlugin branding
- Use your own logo and company name
- Custom cover page
- Suitable for client deliverables

Configure branding under **Accessibility → Settings → Report Branding**.

## Use Cases

### Client Deliverables

Agencies can generate branded reports for each client site, showing current compliance status and recommended fixes.

### Legal Documentation

Keep PDF reports as dated records of your compliance efforts. Useful for:

- EAA compliance documentation
- ADA reasonable accommodation evidence
- Internal audit trails

### Before/After Comparison

Generate reports before and after remediation to demonstrate improvement to stakeholders.
